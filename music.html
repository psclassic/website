Selim's Music

Selim is a passionate musician and composer, performing in a variety of genres including but not limited to classical, jazz, rock, and Turkish classical. His journey in music began at a young age when he started taking piano lessons. Since then, Selim performed in many choirs, ensembles, and concerts. His first composition, "Waltz in a Dream" is streaming on online music platforms. He has published arrangements of many Turkish folk/classical songs, you can check them at  Musescore. You can also watch his live concerts on Instagram, where he shares duo performances with his friend Tuna.
