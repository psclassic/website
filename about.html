About Me

Hi, It’s Selim! I’m a robotics, entrepreneurship, and music enthusiast, who is working his way into the world of computer science. My last initiative was a youth-led NGO that supports high schoolers by bringing them together with professionals, Horizon.

Thanks to coding training at BILSEM, I’ve discovered my interest in computer science, which eventually led me to become a proud founder and captain of the robotics team, FLrobotics. My interests are mainly focused on neural networks, music theory, and neuroscience.

Along with academics, I also enjoy composing music. You can check my works on Spotify. Feel free to connect with me via LinkedIn, I would love to hear about your feedback or discuss collaborations.
